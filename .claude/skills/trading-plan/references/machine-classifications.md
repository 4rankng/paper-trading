# Market Machine Classifications

## HYPE_MACHINE (formerly VOTING_MACHINE)

**Driver:** Social sentiment, retail flow, FOMO, short squeezes

**Strategy:** Trade technicals/sentiment with tight stops

**Timeframe:** 6-month

**Stop Loss:** Technical breakdown (-5% or support break)

**Entry Logic:** Breakout + volume confirmation

**Exit Logic:** Scale out on 20%+ gains

---

## EARNINGS_MACHINE (formerly WEIGHING_MACHINE)

**Driver:** Earnings growth, cash flow compounding, intrinsic value

**Strategy:** Buy undervalued, hold until convergence

**Timeframe:** 3-year+

**Stop Loss:** Fundamental degradation only (ignore price volatility)

**Entry Logic:** < 80% of intrinsic value

**Exit Logic:** Target price / valuation trigger

---

## MEAN_REVERSION_MACHINE (formerly GRAHAM_GAP)

**Driver:** Fundamental Score HIGH, Sentiment Score LOW (Graham Gap)

**Strategy:** Highest conviction BUY, statistical reversal to mean

**Timeframe:** Variable (6-month to 3-year)

**Stop Loss:** Fundamental degradation OR sentiment reversal without price follow-through

**Entry Logic:** Fundamental strength + negative sentiment gap

**Exit Logic:** Sentiment normalization or fundamental validation
